Bang

Bang Pronounced bang

These monsters are large blue or orange creatures with dome-shaped heads and weaponry for hands. They are large and may deliver a stunning blow to a less-experienced adventurer. Be forewarned these masquerpets are capable of aoeing or attacking an area around them so don't get to close to someone else s fight.

Locating Bang

Bang
Bang
A large spawn of Bangs can be found in the Haunt of Nyangnyang just North of the River of Weeping and South of the Mars Mine Dungeon . Second site can be found just north of Sain City.
