Giant Grrr Pronounced g-eye-ant gir

If you've never seen a true troll then you should visit a Grrr. They are large and have dark

green skin. Wearing nothing but a cloth that resembles bandages around it's waist, they sure know how to handle

themselves in battle. They have a red tattoo on both shoulders and carry a huge grungy white axe, that seems to

have red markings in an unknown language. Maybe it's best that you don't get too close trying to read them!
