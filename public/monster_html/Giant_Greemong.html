Giant Greemong Pronounced g-eye-ant grie-mong

Sticky, slimey, gooey, and ooey, these monsters sure know how to gross you out. What looks like was taken straight from a 1950's horror flick is known as Greemong. They are a deep hue of green or brown and have six blue crystals poking upward down their backs. Their just a mucky blob with two arms and no legs. They have crystals for fingers, and red glowing eyes. Wouldn't you hate to get stuck on one of those? As of v10 all Green weapons start dropping from this guy and up.

Locating Giant Greemong

The largest grouping of Greemongs are Southeast of the city of Darkon 2 , in the pools just

under the long natural bridge.
