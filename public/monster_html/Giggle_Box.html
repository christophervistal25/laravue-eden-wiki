Giggle Box

Giggle Box Pronounced gig-gul-bock-ses

Giggle Boxes are light brown and have long arms and legs flailing about as they hobble around. If you ever want to become rich collect all of the nice drops they loose when you defeat them! These tough guys scratch, bite, and slap your greedy paws if you try to find whats hidden in these chests. Wouldn't you giggle too if you had so many amazing goodies stashed away?

Locating Giggle Box
