Nuctuvehicle Pronounced nuk-too-vee-hik-cul

On the deserted tracks in the Eienble Cave live a cluster of Nuctuvehicles. They are not mechanic but get the term 'vehicle' from their choice of transportation, an empty mine cart. Their bodies are made of hard rock and a few crystals, and their eyes glow red. Their arms are very hard and can crush you just as quick as they can ride.

Locating Nuctuvehicle

The best place to find Nuctuvehicles is in Northwestern Darkon 2 just off the western bridge of the town.
