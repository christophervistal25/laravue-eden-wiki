Pinky Roach Pronounced peen-k-roch

In the roach room spawns are a bit different then in the rest of Madrigal. As you enter the room, you will see many clusters of eggs. The small eggs are opened by clicking on them and they contain 3 Roach Larva, 2 agrovated and 1 not. Roachs are a normal spawn. Roach Queens are in the large eggs and they are only openable with a speacial quest item called Wake Roach Water . This item is obtained in the Death of mine part of the Dekane Mines Quest .
