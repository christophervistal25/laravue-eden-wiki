Chimeradon Pronounced Kai-mare-a-don

Flying heavily is the Chimeradon. They have large wings and no eyes! Their bodies are similar to a man with large reptillian feet. They have a large mouth and spikes down their backs like an extinct dinosaur. These godzillas vary from dark green and light green to dark blue and purple.

Locating Chimeradon

Chimeradons can be found in Southern Darkon 3 , along the Eastern edge of the area called Deadwalderness.
