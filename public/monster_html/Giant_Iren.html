Giant Iren Pronounced g-eye-ant aye-ren

Iren resemble large mosquitos. They are light blue and dark purple in color and have four wings

that buzz quickly as they hover. These bugs may fly, but it is not required to be on a broom to fight one.

Locating Giant Iren

The best place to find Irens is in Northeastern Darkon 3 , in the area called Cendil, near the

Altar of the Ringmaster.
