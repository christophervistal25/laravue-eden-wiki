Basque Pronounced bask

The soldiers of Death Island are most certainly the Basques. They are almost reptillian muscle-men with green and yellow skin. They walk on two feet wich are very much alike a lizards' and hold a large bazooka over their shoulder. The loincloth may charm you but the plasma blast they shoot at intruders won't.

Locating Basque

Basques can be found in the Northeastern section of the Garden of Rhisis an island that's directly north of Saint Morning .
