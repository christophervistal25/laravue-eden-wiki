Mushpang

Mushpang Pronounced ' mush-pang'

Bobbling back and fourth, left and right is the Mushpang. Having no legs does not deter this little forest creature from pounding you in the snot box when needed. On the pink shroom of the Mushpang lay one pair deceiving angry looking eyes. Underneath on the pale yellow skin is the stub of a body where the real eyes hide, protected by it's hood. Since the moss covered Mushpang is so small and defenseless it relies on only its fists and intimidating shroom deco to keep predators away. That includes Vagrants!
