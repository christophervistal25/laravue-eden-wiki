Giant Dump Pronounced g-eye-ant dump

If you're walking in the desert and hear an engine purring, maybe you should run. Dumps are huge

monster trucks with three wheels on each side. They have the face of a bull, and long outreaching arms ready to

take a swipe at you. Painted, there are red or blue flames caressing the sides of these mechanical beasts. The

only ride you'll be getting from these guys, is to lodelight.

Locating Giant Dump

The Dumps can be found in Southeastern Darkon 3 .
