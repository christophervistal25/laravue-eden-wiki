Dumb Bull

Dumb Bull Pronounced dum-bul

There is absolutely nothing dumb about this, rather large, temperamental bull. They vary to gray or brown and have either pink or white tattoos on both shoulders. Their long horns pack a punch when they run for you, and their massive bodies aren't easy to deter. The fancy nose ring hanging on their faces are a great quest item, but good luck trying to receive them from these muscle monsters.

Locating Dumb Bull

Dumb Bull
The two large spawns of Dumb Bulls are in the Southeastern most corner of Saint Morning island. The second spawn is just north of the other.
