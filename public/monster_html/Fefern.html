Fefern

Fefern Pronounced Fef-urn

These monsters are strange plants that have a flower-like head with a large mouth. They walk around with root- like feet and have been known to bite. They are weaker monsters for younger players who are fairly confident to battle, and can be found towards the back of Flaris .

Locating Fefern

Fefern
The most popular grouping of Feferns is in the Northern part of Flaris island. Just East of the river of weeping.
