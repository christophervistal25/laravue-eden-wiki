Giant Hoppre Pronounced g-eye-ant hop-reh

Hoppres are giant versions of grasshoppers. They are green and have large clear wings folded on

their backs. Step back when these guys jump up and kick! When they aren't chirping in the Mushpoie Forest, they

are hopping around waiting for an individual to challange them.

Locating Giant Hoppre

The Hoppres can be found all along the area of Heret, in Northern Darkon 3 . Near the Altar of

the Psykeeper.
