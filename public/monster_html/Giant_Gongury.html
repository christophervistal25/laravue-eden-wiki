Giant Gongury Pronounced g-eye-ant gone-goo-ree

These monsters look like metal armadillos and are plated everywhere. They have red eyes, and hard

shells. Their tails are winds that they move round and round. They are quite small but deliver a powerful blow if

needed.

Locating Giant Gongury

Gonguries can be found in Southern Darkon 3 , just north of the entrance into the House of

Glaphan.
