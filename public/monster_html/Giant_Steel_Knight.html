Giant Steel Knight Pronounced g-eye-ant steel-nite

With every other monster, there is none better described by their own name more then the Steel Knight. Short in height but feisty in personality, these little guys dressed in armor can strike a powerful blow with their mighty jousts. Keep a shield in hand unless you want a new piercing.

Locating Giant Steel Knight

Giant Steel Knight can be found in the Southern most area of Darkon 1 . Just South of Erons Factory .
