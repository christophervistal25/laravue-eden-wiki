Giant Antiquery Pronounced g-eye-ant an-tic-que-ree

Resembling willow trees, Antiquery parade Darkon 3 with their mighty presence. They have dark red

glowing eyes, and an angry expression. They can knock the wind out of someone easily with one of their hanging

branches. Above their eyes are light colored spikes on their brows and the bark color varies from brown to dark

blue. When they congregate together you feel like you've just jumped into a haunted version of Little Red Riding

Hood. Don't get lost in that forest!

Locating Giant Antiquery

Antiqueries can be found in Central Darkon 3, in the area of Aniceknight just north of the Altar

of the Ranger.
