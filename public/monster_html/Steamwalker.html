Steamwalker Pronounced steem-wok-ker

Mechanically engineered robots have taken over Darkon 1, and they are known as Steamwalkers. Bounding around, these monsters are controlled by what seems to be a very happy young man with a large greedy smile. He wears a small hat with goggles fit ontop, and is dressed in casual wear. Something just screams out Beware! as you watch him use the Walker's drill for a left hand, and clamp for a right with that evil grin.

Locating Steamwalker

There are two major spawns of Steamwalkers, the first is just to the West of Erons Factory in Central Darkon 1 . The second, is just directly south from the first one by a small lake next to the mountains.
