Catsy Pronounced cat-see

Looking like mutated cats these menacing little beasts are equipped with blade claws and they aren't afraid to use them. Watch out or they'll make sushi of you.

Locating Catsy

Catsys are found as you enter B1. They are the first mob in this new dungeon. They occupy most of the southern area of the floor.
