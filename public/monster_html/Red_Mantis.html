Red Mantis

Red Mantis Pronounced r-ed-man-tiss

These monsters resemble that of a large red praying mantis. They populate the area along the western cliffs of Saint Morning and can occasionally be aggressive to adventurers. They are a mid level monster and can prove to be a difficult fight for those that are still a bit new to the game.

Locating Red Mantis

Red Mantis
The most popular grouping of Red Mantis is in central Saint Morning just to the West of Pumpkin Town.
