Giant Syliaca Pronounced g-eye-ant sil-lee-aca

Imagine sitting in a boat and reeling in one of these! These guys are huge and have green or blue

scales. Their spiney dorsal fins are aligned up their spines and end in a handsome 'elvis' curl on the top of

their heads and range in yellow or red hues. They have two pectoral fins and their eyes are a cement color. They

swim in shallow waters in Darkon 2.

Locating Giant Syliaca

Syliacas can be found in the pools in Eastern Darkon 2 , just before the entrance into Darkon 1 .
