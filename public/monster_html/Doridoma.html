Doridoma Pronounced dohr-ee-dom-a

These monsters are similar to seahorses, but can be seen bouncing around on their tail as opposed to swimming. They are sometimes territorial, and may be aggressive. They are generally found near water and can be located in Flaris .

Locating Doridoma

Doridoma
The largest spawn of Doridomas can be found to the north of the lake just north of Flaris .
