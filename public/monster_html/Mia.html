Mia

Mia Pronounced mee-ah

These young women were imprisoned by the gods, but they will stop and nothing to gain their freedom and it doesn't matter who is in their way. They populate the Southwestern edge of Saint Morning and can occasionally be aggressive to adventurers. They are a lower level monster in the game, and their recent thefts of important Office Document make them the target of many lower level characters out on quests.

Locating Mia

Mia
There are two major locations where Mias can be found, the first is just North of Pumpkin Town. The second is East of the road going South of the town of Saint Morning just north of where the Lodelight is.
