Giant Bang

Giant Bang Pronounced G-eye-ant Bang

This monster is a large blue or orange creature with dome-shaped head and weaponry for hands. They are large and may deliver a stunning blow to a less-experienced adventurer. Be forewarned these masquerpets are capable of aoeing or attacking an area around them so don't get to close to someone else s fight.

Locating Giant Bang
