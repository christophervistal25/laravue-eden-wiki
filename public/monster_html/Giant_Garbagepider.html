Giant Garbagepider Pronounced g-eye-ant gar-bidge-pie-dur

They creepily crawl in Darkon 1 with spider-like mechanical legs. They have a clamp for a hand at

the center of their bodies and range from gray and blue to purple and gold. They are made from metal and the body

seems to be some sort of large tin that resembles a trash can. At the base of their bodies stick out three pointy

spider legs. They walk pretty fast and can't be outrun easily.

Locating Giant Garbagepider

Garbagepiders can be found in Western Darkon 1 , just south of the Dekane Mine .
