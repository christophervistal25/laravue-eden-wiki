Giant Watangka Pronounced g-eye-ant wot-tong-kuh

Watangkas are humble creatures that live in the quiet parts of Darkon 3. They are about as tall

as a normal character and hunch over a bit like cave men. Their skin tone is a dark tan and they wear what seems

to be tribal loin cloths and jewlery on their necks and each wrist. They also wear ceremonial tiki masks over

their faces. If you closely watch them while they walk back and forth, they sometimes randomly break out in a

small tiki dance!

Locating Giant Watangka

Watangkas can be found all around Central Darkon 3 , in the area of Aniceknight.
