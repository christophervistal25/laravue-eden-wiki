Giant Nyangnyang

Giant Nyangnyang Pronounced G-eye-ant nee-ang-nee-ang

This monster is a scantily dressed cat-like creature, and attacks with her sharp claws. This is a formidable foe for more confident new players and can be found towards the back of Flaris .

Locating Giant Nyangnyang

Giant Nyangnyang
The Giant Nyangnyang can be found in Northern Flaris to the North of the bridge crossing the River of Weeping.
