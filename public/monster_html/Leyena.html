Leyena Pronounced lie-yeen-ah

It's highly suggested you keep your distance from these hyenas. They vary a golden brown or copper red with spots here and there, and carry a white mowak down their backs. These guys are pretty tough and have pink tattoos on each shoulder. When you hear their bark, remember their bite is much worse.

Locating Leyena

Leyenas can be found towards the Eastern coast of Darkon 1 .
