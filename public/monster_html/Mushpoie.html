Mushpoie Pronounced moosh-poy

Moshpoies are large mushrooms that walk on two feet that resemble a root. They have two large

arms with boulder-like hands that pack a powerful critical punch. They have brown bodies and a mushroom for a

head that vary from purple to pink. Moshpoies have dangerous glares with white and green eyes that peirce through

you. On their backs, live four smaller pointed mushrooms that dangle and bounce as they strut back and fourth.

I'd hate to get in their way.

Locating Mushpoie

The most popular grouping of Mushpoie is in Darkon 3 .
