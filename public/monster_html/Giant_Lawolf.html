Giant Lawolf

Giant Lawolf Pronounced g-eye-ant Lah-wolf

This monsters is an oddly shaped wolf and can be quite territorial. He is a formidable foe for vagrants who are fairly confident to battle, and can be found towards the Northern part of Flaris .

Locating Giant Lawolf

Giant Lawolf
Giant Lawolf can be found to the south of the River of Weeping and to the west of it by the Leren Mountains. On the left hand side right before the bridge where Buff Pang stands.
