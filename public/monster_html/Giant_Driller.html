Giant Driller Pronounced g-eye-ant dril-ler

Giant Driller are made of metal and have two large drills where there would be arms, and one to move around on like a Top. They can circle smoothly and each drill swivles it's own independant way. They have red glowing eyes in the center of their bodies and a large crank sitting on their heads. Makes you wonder, who in the world is winding them? Note: He is the third masquerpet encountered in the Moot Arvan dungeon.

Locating Giant Driller

Giant Driller can be found in Northern Darkon 1 , just Northwest of Erons Factory .
