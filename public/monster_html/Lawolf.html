Lawolf

Lawolf Pronounced Lah-wolf

These monsters are oddly shaped wolves and can be quite territorial. They are weaker monsters for vagrants who are fairly confident to battle, and can be found towards the Northern part of Flaris .

Locating Lawolf

Lawolf
Lawolfs can be found to the south of the River of Weeping and to the west of it by the Leren Mountains. On the left hand side right before the bridge where Buff Pang stands.
