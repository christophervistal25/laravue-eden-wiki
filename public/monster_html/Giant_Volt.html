Giant Volt Pronounced g-eye-ant volt

These monsters sure know how to electrify with the Plug for a floating right hand and joust for a

left. They are made of metal and are a type of vehicle machinery. They ride around on two convayerbelts for

feet. They stay low to the ground and swing their weapons around their bodies.
