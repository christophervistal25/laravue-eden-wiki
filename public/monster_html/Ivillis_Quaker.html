Ivillis Quaker Pronounced ih-vhul-lis-kwake-ker

Second giant in this dungeon he is a master of earthquake. His humongous feet will stomp you into the ground. Watch out cause he’s going to rock your world. This dungeon monster is not aggravated.

Locating Ivillis Quaker

Not known to roam this is where they spawn. note: Dungeon Masquerpets are known to bug. They may appear anywhere in dungeon. Locations on navigators are their *normal* spawn area's.
