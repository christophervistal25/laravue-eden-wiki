Flybat

Flybat Pronounced Fl-eye-baht

These less than adorable cousins of the Aibatt, Flybats are slightly more evolved and quite a bit more aggressive. They populate the Midwestern corner of Saint Morning and will sometimes be hostile to lower level adventurers. They are a lower level monster in the game, and are the perfect fight for heroes recently past their first job change.

Locating Flybat

Flybat
The most common spawn for Flybats is south of Sain City and the old Duel Arena. They can be spotted west of the Wagsaac and Mia spawns.
