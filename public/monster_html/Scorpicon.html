Scorpicon Pronounced scor-pee-kon

The exoskeleton of the Scorpicon is a deep copper and their one eye is a golden orange. These large arachnids are fighting machines equipped for war. Not only do they have their quick swinging stinger, but they also strut with two large claws and two excrutiating looking pincers. Never get caught in the path of an angry Scopicon, or it'll be the last thing you ever do.

Locating Scorpicon

The spawn of the Scorpicons is in Western part of the Garden of Rhisis to the north of Saint Morning .
