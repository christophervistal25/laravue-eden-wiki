Giant Mothbee

Giant Mothbee Pronounced G-eye-ant moth-bee

Giant Mothbee appearance consists of yellow jacket bodies and large moth like wings. When it comes to damage, these monsters seem to be underestimated since they do not appear very large. You may want to practice your flying skills before you attempt a go at one of these.

Locating Giant Mothbee

Giant Mothbee
The Giant Mothbee is found all over Flaris and over the ocean separating Flaris and Saint Mornings. He can also be found flying over the west coast of Saint Mornings. He is a very rare find.
