Totemia

Totemia Pronounced to-teh-me-ah

What seems to be a joke in appearance is certainly not funny once it bites you with its dentures for a right hand. Totemias are yellow, tall, and like to spin one of the three sections of its body every which way it feels. Even though they're smiling, be aware: it's not because they like you.

Locating Totemia

Totemia
Totemias can be found in the west of the Southern Hillid Mountains, in a raised plateau in central Saint Morning . The area is on the East side of the path that leads south from town.
