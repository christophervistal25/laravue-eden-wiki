Chef Muffrin Pronounced che-ef muff-rin

This is the wrong chef to send back your food to! What looks like it was once a nice white shirt, is now ripped at the seems on his very muscular shoulders. His skin is beat red and his face expresses so much anger your ancestors feel it. He holds a pot of some yellow mysterious stew and a large spoon in his other hand. On the back of his trousers, there is a utility belt holding various kitchen utensils. You really don't want to dip your finger in this pot before the stew is done!

Locating Chef Muffrin

Muffrins can be found in Southern Darkon 3 , in the Southeastern most point of the area called the Deadwalderness.
