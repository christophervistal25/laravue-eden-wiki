Queen Popcrank Pronounced kwe-een pop-crank

Every child's nightmare has finally taken form of an evil jack-in-the-box clown. His face is white with clown make-up, and his hair is green or yellow. His body is lavished with purple, red, gold, and blue silk. He jumps and spins, and likes to chase down his prey unmercifully. If you ever happen to find one of those gold boxes in Darkon 3, maybe you shouldn't wind the crank!

Locating Queen Popcrank

Popcranks can be found in Southern Darkon 3 , in the Southwestern edge of the area known as the Dead Walderness.
