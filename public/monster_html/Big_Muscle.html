Big Muscle

Big Muscle Pronounced big mus-el

This guy is tough, he is large and in charge of this mini dungeon. Presumed to be much to dangerous for the travailing warrior he was entombed long ago by an ancient race who's last people now stand guard. He is the Boss masquerpet encountered in the Big Thyred dungeon.

Locating Big Muscle

Big Muscle
He is the boss of this mini dungeon. To get to him you must meet (Dradpet Manager) Himadel .
