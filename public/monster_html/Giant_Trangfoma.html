Giant Trangfoma Pronounced g-eye-ant trang-foma

What seems to look like an exterminator is really a monster named Trangfoma. Wearing heavy duty boots and a jacket, it's never known what he truly looks like behind his goggles and mask. He carries a backpack of a sort with two spiral wires attached to the top spouting blue and white electricity. If he isnt intimidating you yet, wait for him to approach you and give you a nice wack in the face with his mace. He isn't exterminating bugs you know, it's you!

Locating Giant Trangfoma

Trangfomas can be found in Eastern Darkon 1 near the coast which is directly adjacent to Western Flaris .
