Burudeng Pronounced "ber-rood-deng"

These monsters are large beetles with six legs and large horns and wings. They stalk around around the bridge to the Demian town in Flaris and can occasionally be temperamental. They are the third monsters new players to the game will encounter.

Locating Burudeng

Burudeng
The Burudengs are a very spread out mob with many different spawns in a lot of places. The first spawn, is just west of the path leading north of Flaris . The second spawn is just west of that, near a break in the Leren Mountains. The third spawn is just south of the bridge to Madren Town .
