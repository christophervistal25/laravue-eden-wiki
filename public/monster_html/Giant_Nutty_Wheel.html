Giant Nutty Wheel Pronounced g-eye-ant nut-tee-weel

Made from perhaps chrome or steel, the Giant Nutty Wheel is plated with armor. It has no arms or body, but uses it's long thin legs to kick off the ground for a smooth ride on the large wheel they support themselves on.

Locating Giant Nutty Wheel

The most popular grouping of Nutty Wheels is in the Southeastern edge of Darkon 1 .
