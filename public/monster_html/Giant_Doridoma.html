Giant Doridoma Pronounced g-eye-ant dohr-ee-dom-a

This monster is similar to a seahorse, but can be seen bouncing around on its tail as opposed to swimming. It is sometimes territorial, and is aggressive.

Locating Giant Doridoma

Giant Doridoma
