Pukepuke Pronounced ' pyook-pyook'

These monsters vaguely resemble goblin-like creatures and often carry small knives and ridiculous hats. They inhabit the middle of Flaris and can occasionally be agressive to a young adventurer. They are a relatively easy monster for newer characters in the game.
