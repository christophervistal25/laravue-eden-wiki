Ivillis Leanes Pronounced ih-vhul-lis-lee-nus

Ivillis Leanes is the boss of Ivillis Dungeon and the last part of the Ivillis Temple Quest. He is large and in charge. He is impervious to all Magician spells. But can be affected by Elemental Upgrading on suits and weapons. He will spawn Ivilles Dandysher while you attack him. Evilness incarnate, entombed in his small prison here he hides from all that would blame him. Marked criminal, avenge the poor souls he bewitched. For Shadia, free this pitiless being from his plight. This dungeon monster is not aggravated.

Locating Ivillis Leanes

He is accessed by talking to NPC Darz in the last room of the Dungeon. You must be in a party and must give him the 3 rubies, sorrow, anger and joy. The party members can also not be any higher then lvl 75.
