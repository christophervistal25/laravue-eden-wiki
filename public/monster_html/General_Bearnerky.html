General Bearnerky Pronounced ge-ner-al bare-ner-kee

Beware! This isn't your friendly fire prevention bear! Bearnerky may be cute but hes very ferocious and dangerous. His soft thick fur is brown and he wears two cuffs around each wrist. His small bear claws do plenty damage when combined with his grizzly teeth. If he's running up to you arms open, he isn't trying to give you a hug!

Locating General Bearnerky

The largest grouping of Bearnerkys can be found in Southeastern Darkon 3 , on the Eastern edge of the area known as the Deadwalderness.
