Elderguard Pronounced el-der-gard

These guys seem to be Darkon 2's rendition of a Tinman, nifty bow tie included. They tower over

you two times your own height, and are comepletely plated with armor. Some are blue and white, while others are

black and gray. They have two large claws that they can strike unmercifully if you're bold enough to walk close.

Locating Elderguard

The Elderguards can be found in Western Darkon 1 near the entrance into Darkon 2 just to

the west of Dekane Mine .
