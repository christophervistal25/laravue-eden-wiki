Giant Leyena Pronounced g-eye-ant lie-yeen-ah

It's highly suggested you keep your distance from these hyenas. They vary a golden brown or copper red with spots here and there, and carry a white mowak down their backs. These guys are pretty tough and have pink tattoos on each shoulder. When you hear their bark, remember their bite is much worse. Note: Two of these stand guard in the Moot Arvan dungeon.

Locating Giant Leyena

Leyenas can be found towards the Eastern coast of Darkon 1 .
