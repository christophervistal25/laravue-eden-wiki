Carrierbomb Pronounced care-rear-bom

Carrierbombs are plated with metal from head to toe and have a clock in the center of their

chests. They are short but very agile. they hold a cart attached to their backs filled with many bombs and TNT

sticks. They old a bomb in their right hands, and a TNT in their left. Be careful if you wander by; they throw

these weapons of destruction on target.

Locating Carrierbomb

The largest grouping of Carrierbombs can be found just East of the town of Darkon 2 , just

across the Eastern bridge.
