Ivillis Red Otem Pronounced ih-vhul-lis-red-oh-tem

You are not dreaming, yes you have seen him before. This is the dungeon version of the totomia. If you can cut him down he will fill your pockets. This dungeon monster is aggravated.

Locating Ivillis Red Otem

Not known to roam this is where they spawn. note: Dungeon Masquerpets are known to bug. They may appear anywhere in dungeon. Locations on navigators are their *normal* spawn area's.
