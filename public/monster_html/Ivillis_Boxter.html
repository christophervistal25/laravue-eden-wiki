Ivillis Boxter Pronounced ih-vhul-lis-boks-tir

Like his cousin, he giggles, at the thought of you defeating him. His wears are many but if you sift though the garbage you may find a prize. For it is whispered throughout the land of Madrigal that his duty is guardian of the infamous Ivillis weapons. This dungeon monster is not aggravated, there is one exception.

Locating Ivillis Boxter

Not known to roam this is where they spawn. note: Dungeon Masquerpets are known to bug. They may appear anywhere in dungeon. Locations on navigators are their *normal* spawn area's.
