Risem Pronounced riz-ehm

Made from hard rock, these guys have a certain aura of wonder around them. They float over thin

air with a green glow beneath them the shape of a mysterious pentagram. Instead of arms, they carry two large

boulders ready for slamming and crushing. From their toned abs to their tough arms, it seems a Risem will be

interesting to challange.

Locating Risem

The largest spawn of Risems are in the Westernmost edge of Darkon 2 , just before the entrance into Darkon 3 .
