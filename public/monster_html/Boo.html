Boo Pronounced boo

Boos are large flowers that walk around on their small roots. The pistil of the flower is the

Boo's body, and the petals frame the base like a little tutu. Their arms are the long stamens that hang high over

their bodies. What looks like a fly trap on the top of the pistil, is really the Boo's mouth. They look like

zombie plants if you look close enough and vary colors from yellow and red, to yellow and green.

Locating Boo

Boos can be found in the swamps of Central Darkon 3 near the Altar of Billposter.
