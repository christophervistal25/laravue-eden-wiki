Zombiger Pronounced Zohm-big-ger

Makes you wonder if Eienble Cave has a history of catostrophic events that had to do with Mines doesn't it? Zombigers are truly the minors of Darkon 2 and wear their helmets proudly. They have Goulish blue skin and their bodies are torn. Zombies by nature, they like to slowly hover here or there ( considering they have no legs ) and hold onto their mining tool, a pickaxe. Keep in mind, that pickaxe can easy turn into a weapon if provoked!

Locating Zombiger

Zombigers can be found just off the Northwestern bridge of the city of Darkon 2 .
