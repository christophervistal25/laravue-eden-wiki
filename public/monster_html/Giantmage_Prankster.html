Giantmage Prankster Pronounced g-eye-ant-ma-ge prank-stir

Classified as the magicians of the monster world, they aren't afraid to put a spell on you. Standing tall Pranksters are dressed in gold, red and purple silk with long, droopy pointed hats. They hold onto a long staff with a dark violet orb decorated on the top. Be careful, for they are too proud to be easily defeated.

Locating Giantmage Prankster

The most popular grouping of Pranksters is at the center of the Garden of Rhisis , an island to the north of Saint Morning
