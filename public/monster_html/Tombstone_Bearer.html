Tombstone Bearer Pronounced toom-stone bear-ir

Tombstone Bearers are strong and carry a large heavy obelisk over their shoulders. They have dark blue skin, and wear what seems to be a handsome loincloth. If you dare come near: prepare to be crushed unmercifully over and over, those huge muscles aren't just for show!
