Giant Demian Pronounced g-eye-ant-dee-myan

These monsters are supposedly some sort of outcasts living in Flaris , and often cause a bit of mischeif wherever they are found. They can be particularly mischevious if encountered by an adventurer and may attack suddenly, but are not dangerous to a more experienced player.=
