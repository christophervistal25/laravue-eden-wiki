Kalgas Cave Dungeon

To enter here is pure folly. It is believed that this cave is actually the long lost lair of Kalgas the god of wrath. Will he let loose his anger on Madrigal and final give Shade what she has so long sought. Revenge! For those brave or stupid enough to venture into these depths the perils shall be great but the rewards shall be greater.

Kalgas Cave Dungeon is accessible from the Bahara Desert continent.

Dungeon Particularities

All Masquerpets are aggravated. This is an Instance Dungeon so only Party and or Guild members will be present.

Certain rules apply:

1) You must be in a party to enter dungeon but the party is not teleported to the dungeon if you enter it. You may proceed alone, though this is not advised. 2) If you leave the Dungeon you must wait for 360 minutes before you can re-enter ( cooldown ). 3) If you disconnect you will be spawned just outside the dungeon and re-entrance is permitted as long as at least one member of your Party remains in the instance. 4) You must kill 6 statues before (God of Wrath) Kalgas spawns.

  • Balt Statue
  • Akin Statue
  • Kalgas Statue x4

5) The use of any Transy item (e.g. Aibatt Trans ) is no longer allowed and possible within the dungeons. Furthermore players using a Transy item will be unable to enter the dungeon. 6) You must be a level 135 Hero or higher to enter this dungeon. 7) (God of Wrath) Kalgas does not drop full sets. He will only drop pieces .
