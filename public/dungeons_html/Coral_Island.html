Coral Island Dungeon

Come discover the beautiful Isle that is Coral Island. This lovely new area offers you double experience and booty but to gain access one must hold a rare orange ticket. Purchasable from the , these little stubs shall soon be in large demand by your fellow Madrigolians. Coral Island Ticket History Reserved for ages for only the more financially endowed this resort quality beach front land is now on the market. Come and "own", for a time, your part of the beach. A new land has been discovered in the world of Madrigal. Coral Island the great beach oasis. Monsters on Coral Island range from levels 43-69 and give 2x more experience then regular monsters. All monsters on Coral Island are non aggressive and the spawns are big, good for 1vs1 and AoE. Suggested Level: 42+

Coral Island is seen as its own continent.

Dungeon Particularities

Some Masquerpets are aggravated. This is not an Instance Dungeon so other players will be present.
Entrance Fee

To enter here one must have a a Coral Island Ticket . There are three kinds of tickets, one lasting 1 day (28 Wcoins) and the other for 15 days (210 Wcoins). They can be found in the , private shops in-game and in certain in game events.
