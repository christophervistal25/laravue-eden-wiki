�Was it ever real? Everything was so strange, I was dwarfed by plants, the flora and the fauna were literally trying to kill me�but then I awoke�Euphresia was just like a dream-�

This fantastical new dungeon is one of dreamlike wonders. Step into this land to find yourself traversing a jungle of mushrooms, flowers, and battling the beautiful yet poisonous denizens that reside there. You may or may not be able to tell what�s real; are those flowers pretty? Or are they trying to bring an end to your adventures once and for all? Should you feel strong enough to brave through such things that could only be in a dream, you will eventually face the ruler of this would be nightmare � The Queen of Thorns Entaness. It is said that she is very powerful, but that she keeps precious rare artifacts and weapons; only those able to defeat her may discover these treasures.

Euphresia the Isle of Dreams and Euphresia the Isle of Nightmares are mid level dungeons designed for players levels 75-170H, and players level 75M-170H respectively. Both instances are located to the North of Darkon 3.

Isle of Dreams Dungeon is accessible from the Darkon continent.

Dungeon Particularities

All Masquerpets are aggravated. This is an Instance Dungeon so only Party and or Guild members will be present.
Entrance Fee
While no fee is required, players must be level 75 and up.

Certain rules apply:

1) You must be in a party to enter dungeon but party is not teleported to dungeon if you enter it. You may proceed alone, though this is not advised

2) You must be level 75 and up to enter this dungeon.

3) If you leave the Dungeon you must wait 90 minute cooldown time to re-enter.

4) If you DC you will be spawned just outside the dungeon and you cannot re-enter unless a party member is still in the dungeon.

5) The use of Trans, be it Aibatt Trans or other, are no longer allowed within the dungeons

Monsters Present

This dungeon is for level 75 ~ 170H Masquerpets. This is a party dungeon so masquerpets hit hard and have a lot of hp. Rewards are very interesting. Here is where you will find the Ultimate Phantasm Weapons

Dungeon Prizes

All Ultimate Phantasm Weapons are level 80 and are Soul Linked. Meaning once they have been equipped they may not be banked, warehoused, traded or sold. They may be put in backpack. They are not all restricted to second class. For example, wands and staffs can be equipped by Magicians while others like the Bow can be equipped by Rangers only.
