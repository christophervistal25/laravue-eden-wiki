Azria Dungeon

Come discover the beautiful frigid isle that is Azria. This lovely new area offers you double experience and booty but to gain access one must hold a rare green ticket. Purchasable from the , these little stubs shall soon be in large demand by your fellow Madrigolians. Azria Ticket History Long before man recorded time, there existed a land as cold as the heart of Iblis. This land was covered in ice and snow. Before the great war of the Hero's all access to this land was lost. Its memory having not been recorded, the knowledge of its existence was lost. A new land has been discovered in the world of Madrigal. Azria the great land of ice. Monsters in Azria range from levels 73-115 and give 20% more experience then regular monsters. All monsters in Azria are non aggressive and the spawns are big, good for 1vs1 and AoE.

Azria is seen as its own continent.

Dungeon Particularities

Some Masquerpets are aggravated. This is not an Instance Dungeon so other players will be present.
Entrance Fee

To enter here one must have a an Azria Ticket . There are three kinds of tickets, one lasting 1 day (35 Wcoins) one last for 7 days (175 Wcoins) and the other for 15 days (280 Wcoins). They can be found in the , private shops in-game and in certain in game events.
