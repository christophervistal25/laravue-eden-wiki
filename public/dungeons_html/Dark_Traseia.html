Dark Traseia Dungeon

Come discover the sinister Isles that are Dark Traseia. This dreadful new area offers you double experience and booty but to gain access one must hold a rare purple ticket. Purchasable from the , these little stubs shall soon be in large demand by your fellow Madrigolians. Dark Traseia Ticket History Once a piece of Roika that was torn asunder by Shade during the War of the Gods, this grouping of islands has drifted aimlessly through time. The fiercest of Shades minions, the Terrorpin Tribe, invaded this land at Her command long before the conclusion of the War. The Terrorpins had no trouble establishing dominance over the peaceful inhabitants of this land. The rabbit-like natives were not accustomed to war and were caught largely unaware and completely unprepared for the invasion on their home front. Only the strongest and most brave of the rabbit natives remain, and even then, they are only able to carve out one small isle for their own. Tempered by their experience, the Sakai are no longer welcoming visitors with open arms.

Dungeon Particularities

Some Masquerpets are aggravated. This is not an Instance Dungeon so other players will be present.
Entrance Fee

To enter here one must have a a Dark Traseia Ticket . There are three kinds of tickets, one lasting 1 day (60 Wcoins), 7 days (300 Wcoins) and 15 days (500 Wcoins). They can be found in the , private shops in-game and in certain in game events.
