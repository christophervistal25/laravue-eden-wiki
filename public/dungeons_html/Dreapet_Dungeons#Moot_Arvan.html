Big Thyred

History A long long time ago, there was very a famous adventurer named Big Thyred. One day, he found an interesting place to adventure� It was a cave deep underground, surrounded with this strange, bloody fog. Big Thyred investigated the cave only to become infected by a disease within the cave called Horrible Fear. The disease transformed him into a hideous monster, whom we now call Big Muscle. There's virtually nothing left of that brave adventurer but bits and pieces, or so the story goes�

Big Thyred is accessible from the Flaris continent.

Dungeon Particularities

All Masquerpets are aggravated. This is an Instance Dungeon so only Party and or Guild members will be present.
