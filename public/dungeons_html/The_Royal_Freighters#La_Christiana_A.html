Certain rules apply:

1) You must be in a party to enter dungeon but party is not teleported to dungeon if you enter it. You may proceed alone, though this is not advised

2) You must be level 105 and up to enter this dungeon.

3) If you leave the Dungeon you must wait 90 minute cooldown time to re-enter.

4) If you DC you will be spawned just outside the dungeon and re-entrance is permitted

5) The use of Trans, be it Aibatt Trans or other, are no longer allowed within the dungeons

6) You must kill the Phalanx for the Silverwing Dutchess to appear.

Monsters Present

This dungeon is for level 105 ~ 150H Masquerpets. This is a party dungeon so masquerpets hit hard and have a lot of hp. Rewards are very interesting. Here is where you will find the Les Britannia S Weapons

Les Britannia S&La Christiana A
  • Mouzer lvl 105
  • Winged Mouzer lvl 106
  • Regal Hawk lvl 107
  • Regal Raptor lvl108
  • Crimson Garuda lvl 109
  • Veiled Garuda lvl 110
  • Phalanx lvl111
  • Silverwing Dutchess lvl 116

Dungeon Prizes

All Les Britannia S Weapons are level 110 and are Soul Linked. Meaning once they have been equipped they may not be banked, warehoused, traded or sold. They may be put in backpack. They are not all restricted to second class. For example, wands and staffs can be equipped by Magicians while others like the Bow can be equipped by Rangers only.
