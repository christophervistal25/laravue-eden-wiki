Catacombs of Anguish Dungeon

This is the new Hero instanced Dungeon! An Instance Dungeon is a dungeon that is made just for you and your party. You must be in a party to participate in these dungeons. Because the Dungeon is made for you and your party, you will be the only ones present within the dungeon. ALL Masquerpets are aggravated. History Deep in the Valley of the Risen, a natural cave has been discovered. Many adventurers have set out to explore this mysterious cave, never to return. Entire search parties have never been seen again. Some say this cave is the entrance to a crypt, where cursed souls were banished by Rhisis long ago. Are you brave enough to enter such a place?

Catacombs of Anguish Dungeon is accessible from the Valley of the Risen continent.

Dungeon Particularities

All Masquerpets are aggravated. This is an Instance Dungeon so only Party and or Guild members will be present.

Certain rules apply:

1) You must be in a party to enter dungeon but party is not teleported to dungeon if you enter it. You may proceed alone, though this is not advised. 2) If you leave the Dungeon you must wait 180 minute cooldown time to re-enter. 3) If you DC you will be spawned just outside the dungeon and re-entrance is permitted. 4) Although clearing the Dungeon is preferred, only the 5 Idols are necessary kills to spawn the boss (Deathbringer) Kheldor. 5) The use of Trans, be it Aibatt Trans or other, are no longer allowed within the dungeons. 6) You must be a level 121 Hero to enter dungeon.

Monsters Present

This dungeon is for level Masquerpets. This is a party dungeon so masquerpets hit hard and have a lot of hp. Rewards are very interesting. Here is where you will find the Dryad Armor

  • Fallen Necromancer
  • Forsaken Banshee
  • (Deathbringer) Kheldor
